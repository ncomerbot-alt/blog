The Origin Story

It started, as most good stories do, with a sauna.

There's this place in Helsinki called Sompasauna. It's beautiful, public, the kind of spot that feels like a secret only locals know about. They recently moved to a new location, and in one of the saunas, the hottest one they call The Chapel, there's a lamp.

Not just any lamp. A lamp that tells you, in real time, the temperature of the sauna.

I stood there, sweat dripping, staring at this glowing indicator, and thought: "I need this in my kitchen."

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 curious mind
  • A pinch of JavaScript

Method:

  1. Ask the API for the current price
  2. Apply color logic (green/yellow/orange/red)
  3. Serve immediately
